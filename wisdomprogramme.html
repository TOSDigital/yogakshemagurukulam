Indic Wisdom Programme

While knowledge is simply acquiring information, wisdom involves a deeper understanding that shapes our perspective on life and the world around us. India was once a land of profound wisdom, which was passed down through generations until the pursuit of knowledge for jobs and bread took over. At Yogakshema, we believe that education should be an enjoyable process, rather than a stressful academic exercise. That's why our department of Indic Wisdom is dedicated to reviving the lost wisdom system by offering practical, logical, and applicable insights from scriptural wisdom through online and offline workshops, seminars, retreats, and courses.

At the core of Indic wisdom lies the fundamental unity underlying all existence, and the ultimate aim of human life is to realize this unity through wisdom. This goal is expressed in various ways in different Indic Scriptures, all emphasizing self-transformation and inner peace and harmony. Our program delves into the key teachings and concepts of Indic wisdom, drawing from sources such as the Vedas, Upanishads, Bhagavad Gita, Yoga Sutras, and other classical texts. We explore how these teachings can be applied to modern life, helping us to lead more meaningful and fulfilling lives. Join us at Yogakshema and discover the richness of Indic wisdom, and how it can positively transform your life.
