Dr. Anantha Sharma B G

DIRECTOR OF INDIC WISDOM

Born in ChannarayaPatna, Hassan district of Karnataka, he underwent 12 years of traditional Gurukul system of education in Prabodhini Gurukulam, Hariharapura and Veda Vijnana Gurukulam, Bengaluru. Later, he acquired in-depth knowledge of Indian shastras under the total age of various renowned scholars. He has been an explorer in the field of ancient wisdom, especially Vedānta and Yoga. He has taught shastras in Veda Vijnana Gurukulam and Maitreyee Gurukulam, and also has delivered several lectures and invited talks on various different platforms.

He has been active in academics and has presented papers in several conferences. He has been a resource person in several workshops organised by various institutions. His experience with students has led him to explore the applicability of shastras for different stages of student life and also for the related professions. He keeps his interest in a trans disciplinary approach towards our ancient knowledge resources.

Dr. Gayathri Melnad

DIRECTOR OF INDIC HEALTH

Gayathri is A Naturopath & Yoga trainer by profession, wife of a Vedic scholar and mother of a divine soul who is now 3 years. Having studied under a normal education system, I always felt that education is just creating a void within me rather than filling me with the knowledge that I really need to bring that excellence in me. Somewhere I was missing to connect myself. Then by the age of 18, I happened to get into the world of Yogic lore. It was in itself a journey I had begun where it gave me second birth. All thanks to my brother it was his decision that helped me to begin my life all over again just like how I wanted it to be.

SVYASA was not just another university for the degree, it was more like learning life amidst the spiritual gurus. We studied Yoga Shastras, Bhagavadgeeta, Upanishads along with our medical curriculum. Life in the university taught us to “Be & Make” that’s the mission of the university which was the crux of our education system. After completing my 5 & 1/2 years of education I served the university as Assistant professor for 2 years for BNYS, BSc Yoga & MSc Yoga courses, also I took part in several other health camps conducted by the university.

Leaving the campus after about 9yrs of my life was a tough decision, yet I was ready to face the real world out. Now it was my time to take that first move to come out of the nest where I was all safe and pampered. Looked for an opportunity to work overseas & worked as a yoga trainer in Doha Qatar for a year in an Indian Arts and Music institution. Thereafter returning to India my journey began with my life partner Anantha Sharma, whom I chose for lifetime as a guru to learn and grow with. We both got an opportunity to work in the traditional Indian knowledge system for 4 years till 2023. Recently I moved to Bengaluru, to be part of this new venture Yogakshema. This is another beginning for me to be the Director, Division of Health & Fitness. Being part of Yogakshema is my dream project answered by the universe to bring me into the group headed by Aravind Prasadji.

LIPI PUSHPAKARAN

DIRECTOR OF INDIC HEALING

Mayasandra Krishnaprasad

Director, Dept of Art & Theatre

As a child he always had an unshakeable interest towards theatre arts and was passionate about stage performances. This directed him as a child artist, towards skits wired in All India Radio during the year 1969. That is how his journey began in the field of theatrical arts. His schooling days was filled with memories of awards and recognitions for his unmatchable stage performances. This paved the way for him to be an artist of Benaka group of Amateur Theatre in Bangalore from 1977. This is where he meets well known theatrical scholars from Karnataka like Sri Nagabharana, well-known Actor Sri Sundararaj and other such artists. His valuable experience in the field of theatre was well recognized by Jawaharlal Bal Bhavan, Bangalore and he was appointed as Mentor for division of theatre in their Institute.

On one fine day during the year 1987, Mayasandra Krishna Prasad became a prestigious artist of Rangayana Theatrical Repertory established by Rangabheeshma Sri BV Karanth. Most of the scripts enacted by Mayasandra Krishna Prasad, directed by National and International Directors helped him to learn and improve his skills of excellence on stage. He is one among those skilled and famous artists from Rangayana who have performed Hundreds of plays thousands of times in India, America, Germany and Austria. Apart from this fame, he also is well known for his scripts, direction and performance in theatrical training for kids. He was also one among those script writers for theatrical performance of famous books from prestigious authors like Kuvempu and UR Ananthamurthy.

He has a lot of plays to his credits which were running housefull during those days when theatrical Arts were dominating the Big Screens. You can see his acting skills in small screen Kannada serials like O nanna Belake, Gelati, Doora Teera Yaana, Manvantara and Godhooli. At present he might be a retired senior artist from Rangayana, but he has not retired from his Passion towards teaching theatrical skills to the next generation. To know more about his punctuality, leadership qualities, acting skills and script writing talent, you should be attending one of his courses and training programs organised by Yogakshema.
