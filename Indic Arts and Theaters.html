Indic Arts & Theatre

Theatre, Culture and Arts have contributed a lot towards skill development, nurturing passion, spreading entertainment and sculpturing personalities in Ancient India. Cave paintings, temple art, music, dance, puppetry, street theatre, royal theatre and various other art forms were all testaments of rich culture, art and theatre which are evolving till date.

Younger generation today is more attracted towards cinemas than theatre and other art forms. Seldom have the young minds recognized that many popular actors and artists in cinema have a strong foundation of learning from a traditional mentor or Guru. Many young minds today are passionate about different art forms and are also in search of traditional teachers who can help them build a career out of Arts and Theatre. Hence; Art, Music, Dance and Theatre taught at Yogakshema Gurukulam, will play a major role in maintaining social unity, cultural wisdom, recording historical events and creating a stage for the young minds of the present generation to unite their passion and profession. Theatre can contribute a lot in reviving lost art forms, imbibing socio-cultural values, supporting physical and mental well-being of society that is busy running behind name and fame. To experience the wonderful impact of Art and Theatre in your life, all you need is an interest and passion towards self-development. So choose a program from our list and join us soon to learn more about art and Theatre.
