Gurukulam

Ancient India was renowned for its vast treasury of wisdom and spirituality, with the gurukulas standing out as prestigious centers of knowledge and research. In those times, every village, house, and public gathering spot was an opportunity to learn and grow. This resulted in an astounding 732,000 gurukulas that existed before the adoption of modern teaching methods in the country.

Gurukulams were once exclusive hubs of wisdom, wellness, health, and healing, with every citizen of the land eager to learn from these fountains of knowledge. Sadly, those days and memories are now just a fading memory. Today, Gurukulam for the Modern World has been reduced to a religious space rather than a center of learning. The general perception of Gurukulam is often that of a bearded man seated under a banyan tree teaching Vedas to his disciples. However, the modern mind has become increasingly ignorant or even repulsive towards Indic wisdom, wellness, health, and healing methods that have been passed down to us through various means.

Indic Wisdom

While knowledge is simply acquiring information, wisdom involves a deeper understanding that shapes our perspective on life and the world around us. India was once a land of profound wisdom, which was passed down through generations until the pursuit of knowledge for jobs and bread took over.

Indic Wellness

Living a consciously chosen lifestyle is essential for achieving wellness. It requires making conscious choices about our education, health, healing, and lifestyle. Wellness is not just about physical health but encompasses all aspects of our lives. The Indic Wellness System has a strong philosophical foundation that considers the social, emotional, financial, professional, spiritual, and relational aspects of life.

Indic Health

The Indic Health System asserts that illness in our body and mind stems from Nature, Ignorance, Desires, and Unconscious Action. Following the prescribed routines of Indic seasons, festivals, and everyday life can promote ease and well-being in our body and mind. These routines are collectively known as Yoga, encompassing any life habits that can create ease and keep dis-ease at bay.

Indic Healing

The Indic Healing System is an ancient field of research and practice that dates back many thousands of years. The development of Astrological Healing was guided by the Rig Veda, while the Atharva Veda served as the guiding force for other healing philosophies and practices originating from India, such as Ayurveda and Tantric healing methods. These methodologies play a vital role in the development of the Indic Healing System as a comprehensive and holistic system of practice, based on the principle of balance and harmony between the physical, subtle, and spiritual dimensions of existence.

Indic Arts & Theatre

Theatre, Culture and Arts have contributed a lot towards skill development, nurturing passion, spreading entertainment and sculpturing personalities in Ancient India. Cave paintings, temple art, music, dance, puppetry, street theatre, royal theatre and various other art forms were all testaments of rich culture, art and theatre which are evolving till date.
