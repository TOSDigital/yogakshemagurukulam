Indic Wellness Programme

Living a consciously chosen lifestyle is essential for achieving wellness. It requires making conscious choices about our education, health, healing, and lifestyle. Wellness is not just about physical health but encompasses all aspects of our lives. The Indic Wellness System has a strong philosophical foundation that considers the social, emotional, financial, professional, spiritual, and relational aspects of life. This holistic approach creates a platform for health, harmony, and happiness.

Indic Wellness is a combination of Indic Wisdom, Indic Health, and Indic Healing systems that help us achieve internal and external wellbeing. Our beliefs, ideologies, and understanding of ourselves and the world around us play a crucial role in creating this space of wellbeing. Modern technology has positively impacted our lives but has also had a negative impact on individual and social well being.
At Yogakshema, our Department of Wellness focuses on the most important yet simple aspects of modern human problems and provides solutions based on the Indic Wellness Philosophy and Practices. Our workshops, seminars, retreats, and online courses provide practical, logical, and applicable solutions that help individuals achieve a state of conscious living and wellness.
